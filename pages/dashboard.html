Admin Dashboard

User Management
  • View comprehensive lists of patients, doctors, and other admins.
  • Edit user details and update roles or permissions.
  • Delete or suspend user accounts as needed.
Schedule & System Management
  • Update and manage doctors' working hours and availability.
  • Approve or adjust appointment schedules.
  • Configure system settings and manage access levels.
Registration & Administration
  • Create new admin accounts or add new doctor profiles.
  • Manage registration requests and validate required information.
  • Set up policies for secure account creation.
Reports & Analytics
  • View appointment statistics and user activity logs.
  • Generate financial reports and system performance analytics.
  • Monitor usage trends and generate custom reports.

Patient Dashboard

Upcoming Appointments
  • See a list of all your future appointments including date, time, and location.
  • Click an appointment to view details or cancel if needed.
Chatbox & Communication
  • Access your chat history with doctors for quick reference.
  • Initiate a chat with an AI assistant for preliminary advice.
  • Start a live chat with available doctors during working hours.
Personal Profile & Medical Information
  • Review and update your personal details including contact info and address.
  • Check your current medications and diagnostics information.
  • View general health statistics such as weight, height, and more.
Make an Appointment
  • Filter by medical expertise (e.g., cardiology, neurology, etc.).
  • Search for the closest available specialist based on your address.
  • Click on a specialist to view detailed information before booking.

Doctor Dashboard

Your Appointments
  • View a detailed list of your upcoming appointments.
  • See appointment specifics: patient name, time, location, and expertise.
  • Update or cancel appointments directly from your dashboard.
Search Patient information
  • Search for patients using their ID or name.
  • Access detailed patient cards including contact info, age, and current diagnosis.
  • Review past appointment history and medication records.
Update Patient Records
  • Add new medications or update existing treatment plans.
  • Record new diagnoses and treatment adjustments.
  • Maintain an up-to-date medical history for each patient.
Communication & Messaging
  • View messages from patients and respond directly through the chat system.
  • Receive appointment alerts and updates.
  • Integrate with AI tools to streamline routine communications.
